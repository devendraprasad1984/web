<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; text-align: justify; line-height: 12pt; background-image: initial; background-position: initial; background-size: initial; background-repeat: initial; background-attachment: initial; background-origin: initial; background-clip: initial;"><i><span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">&nbsp;Tentative scheme : <o:p></o:p></span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><i><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">A.
PRELIMINARY EXAMINATION :<o:p></o:p></span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><i><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">&nbsp;The Examination shall comprise of two
compulsory Papers of 200 marks each. <o:p></o:p></span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><i><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Note
: (i) Both the question papers will be of the objective type (multiple choice
questions).<o:p></o:p></span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><i><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">&nbsp;(ii) The General Studies Paper-II of the Civil
Services (Preliminary) Examination will be a qualifying paper with minimum
qualifying marks fixed at 33%. <o:p></o:p></span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><i><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">(iii)
The question papers will be set both in Hindi and English. <o:p></o:p></span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><i><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">(iv)
Details of the syllabi are indicated in Part A of Section III.<o:p></o:p></span></i></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><i><span style="font-size: 10pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">&nbsp;(v) Each paper will be of two hours duration.
Blind candidates and the candidates with Locomotor Disability and Cerebral
Palsy where dominant (writing) extremity is affected to the extent of slowing
the performance of function (minimum of 40% impairment) will; however, be
allowed an extra time of twenty minutes per hour for each paper.<u><o:p></o:p></u></span></i></p>
